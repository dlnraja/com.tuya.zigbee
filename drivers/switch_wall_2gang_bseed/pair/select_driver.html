⚠️ BSEED Firmware Bug Warning

Known Issue: This BSEED switch (_TZ3000_l9brjwau) has a firmware bug where both gangs switch together when controlling one gang.

Workaround Enabled: This driver includes automatic correction to restore the opposite gang state after each command.

How to Pair:

  1. Press and hold the power button for 5 seconds
  2. Wait for the LED to flash rapidly
  3. The device will appear in Homey

Bug reported and extensively tested by Loïc Salmona (Nov 2025)
